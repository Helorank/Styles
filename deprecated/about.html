Our Product

Helorank is a new way to track the games you play every day. From golf with coworkers before lunch to pickup basketball after dinner, you're competitive. And when you win, you want people to remember your victory. But memory is faulty. Memory is selective. And the people you beat don't seem to remember at all. That's why we created Helorank.

Create a league with your friends. Submit games after you play them. It's that easy. We'll go over the results and rank you.

Our app is more than a win/loss counter. As you win games, your power rating within a league of friends goes up. But as your score gets bigger, so does the target on your back. Lower-ranked players get a lot more points for taking down league leaders. We don't just sort you by the number of wins and losses. We rank you by the strength of your opponents in addition to game outcomes.

If you want to know how you measure up among your friends, sign up and give Helorank a try. Track your games. Quantify your wins. Helorank your reality.
